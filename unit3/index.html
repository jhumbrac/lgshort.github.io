About Me

The one constant in my career has always been people. Understanding how people think, getting answers to "why" people do what they do, and helping customers explore new ways to improve the user experience. I dabble in digital design, photography, but most importantly, I specialize in user research and content strategy. My goal is to create both digital and print media that people can emotionally connect with for a positive user experience.
